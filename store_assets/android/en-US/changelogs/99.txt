Initial release.

• Dashboard shortcut widget — open any Home Assistant dashboard with one tap
• Multi-entity widget — show and control several entities in one resizable widget, with rows and secondary chips
• Works offline — widgets show the last known state with a staleness marker
• Secure — your access token is stored encrypted on the device and never leaves it
• No cloud — connects directly to your own Home Assistant instance
• Dark, light or system theme, with 6 widget color themes
• Available in English, Danish and Swedish
• Confirm-on-tap option to avoid accidental actions
• Optional diagnostic report you can send if something goes wrong — never sent automatically
